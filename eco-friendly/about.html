About EcoPlate

Our Story

EcoPlate was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to transform waste into sustainable solutions. In 2018, our founders witnessed firsthand the devastating impact of plastic pollution on our oceans and the mounting waste from sugarcane production. This sparked a mission to create a product that could address both issues simultaneously.

Through extensive research and collaboration with environmental scientists and material engineers, we developed our innovative biodegradable plates made from sugarcane bagasse. Today, EcoPlate stands at the forefront of the fight against plastic pollution, offering a sustainable alternative that doesn't compromise on quality or convenience.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To revolutionize the disposable tableware industry by providing eco-friendly, biodegradable alternatives that reduce plastic waste and repurpose agricultural byproducts.

Vision

We envision a world where sustainable choices are the norm, not the exception. EcoPlate aims to be a catalyst in this global shift, inspiring individuals and businesses to embrace environmentally responsible products in their everyday lives.
