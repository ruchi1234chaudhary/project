Our Environmental Impact

How We Make a Difference

At EcoPlate, we're committed to transforming sugarcane waste into sustainable solutions. Here's how our process benefits the environment:

  • Repurposing agricultural waste that would otherwise be burned or left to rot
  • Reducing reliance on petroleum-based plastics
  • Creating fully biodegradable products that leave no harmful residues
  • Minimizing water usage in our production process

Our Future Sustainability Goals

  • Achieve carbon neutrality in our production process by 2025
  • Expand our product line to include cups and cutlery made from agricultural waste
  • Implement a closed-loop water system to reduce water consumption by 80%
  • Partner with 100 more local farms to source sugarcane waste sustainably
  • Invest in research to reduce biodegradation time of our products to 30 days
